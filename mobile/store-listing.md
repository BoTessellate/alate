## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Stop guessing if that top, dress, or pair of jeans will actually fit you.

Alate analyzes clothing from any online store and tells you how it'll fit YOUR body — before you buy. No more returns, no more disappointment.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Set up your body profile (height, proportions — takes 30 seconds)
2. Paste a product URL from any clothing site (ASOS, Zara, H&M, and more)
3. Get instant fit warnings and a fit score

WHAT YOU GET
• Personalized fit score (Great / Moderate / Poor)
• Specific warnings like "May be shorter on you than on model" or "Non-stretch fabric may be snug on curves"
• Works with thousands of online clothing stores
• Save and review your fit check history

SMART FIT ANALYSIS
Alate considers your height, body proportions, the garment category, material, and fit style to generate predictions tailored to you. Our analysis covers common fit issues like:
- Length mismatches for tall or petite frames
- Stretch vs. non-stretch fabric on different body types
- Oversized vs. fitted styles on various proportions
- Cropped items on taller figures

PRIVACY FIRST
• Your body data stays on your device by default
• Optional Google sign-in to sync across devices
• No ads, no tracking, no data selling
• You can delete all your data at any time

SHARE TO CHECK
Share a product link directly from your browser or shopping app to Alate — no need to copy and paste.

Built for real bodies. Shop with confidence.
